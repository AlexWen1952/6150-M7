Understanding Glaucoma

What is Glaucoma?

Glaucoma is a group of eye conditions that damage the optic nerve, typically due to increased pressure in the eye.

Recognizing Glaucoma

Glaucoma often has no symptoms in its early stages. Regular eye check-ups are crucial for early detection.

Care for People with Glaucoma

Individuals with glaucoma should follow their doctor's recommendations, take prescribed medications, and attend regular eye exams.
